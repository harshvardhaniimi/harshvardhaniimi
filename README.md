### Present
I'm an Assistant Professor of Information Systems and Analytics at the [School of Business Administration](https://www.aus.edu/sba), American University of Sharjah. I teach Python for Business Analytics, Data Mining and Machine Learning, and Introduction to Management Information Systems.

My research sits at the intersection of machine learning, applied statistics, and business decision-making. I work on problems in retail analytics, AI in higher education, and how organisations respond to instability, with recent submissions to *Expert Systems with Applications* and work in progress for *California Management Review*.

I completed my PhD in [Business Analytics and Statistics](https://haslam.utk.edu/business-analytics-statistics) at the Haslam College of Business, University of Tennessee. Before that, I was an intern with HP Inc.'s Strategic Planning and Modelling Team, where I built [ML forecasting models](https://www.harsh17.in/hp22/) for demand prediction of 18,000+ products 170+ countries (see [Harshvardhan et al.](https://pubsonline.informs.org/doi/10.1287/inte.2024.0126)). I completed my BA and MBA at [IIM Indore](https://iimidr.ac.in) in April 2021, and was an ERASMUS+ exchange student at the [University of Latvia](https://www.lu.lv/en/) from Feb-Jun 2019.
